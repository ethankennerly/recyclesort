Recycle Sort

Press LEFT or RIGHT arrow to sort garbage.

Score points for saving money in San Francisco.

Recognize the opportunity for the item:

Landfill:
Plastic bag:  7
Styrofoam:  2

Recycle:
Aluminum can:  8
Plastic bottle:  1

To save the most:  reduce and reuse.
Reducing can save 100x as much as recycling.
Reusing can save 10x as much as recycling.

Weekend game jam by Ethan Kennerly.
Inspired by Speed Sort on Fit Brains.
