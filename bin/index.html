Recycle Sort

Press LEFT or RIGHT arrow to sort garbage.

Score points for saving money for your community.

Recognize which saves the biggest opportunity for the item:
Electronics:  Center.  100 cents.
Aluminum can:  Recycle.  10 cents.
Styrofoam:  Landfill.  10 cents.
Soiled paper:  Landfill.  2 cents.
Bakeware:  Landfill.  1 cents.
Mylar:  Landfill.  1 cents.
Plastic bag:  Landfill.  1 cents.
Plastic bottle:  Recycle.  1 cent.

To save even more money:  reduce, reuse, recycle.
Reducing can save 100x as much as recycling.
Reusing can save 10x as much as recycling.
